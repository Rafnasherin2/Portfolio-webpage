Iâ€™m Anushka Sharma, born 1 May 1988, an Indian actress who works in Hindi films. Over the years, I have received several awards, including a Filmfare Award. As of 2018, I am one of the highest-paid actresses in India. Since 2012, I have appeared in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 and was featured in Forbes Asia's 30 Under 30 list in 2018.

I was born in Ayodhya and raised in Bangalore. My first modelling assignment was for the fashion designer Wendell Rodricks in 2007, after which I moved to Mumbai to pursue a full-time career as a model. I made my acting debut opposite Shah Rukh Khan in the top-grossing romantic film Rab Ne Bana Di Jodi (2008). I rose to prominence with starring roles in Yash Raj Films' romances Band Baaja Baaraat (2010) and Jab Tak Hai Jaan (2012), winning the Filmfare Award for Best Supporting Actress for the latter.
